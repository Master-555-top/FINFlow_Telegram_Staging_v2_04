# CALENDAR / TIME IMPACT PLANNER

## Purpose
Real life tasks consume time and affect taxi income, fatigue, and feasibility. Calendar is part of financial planning.

## Event fields
- title
- date
- start/end or duration
- category
- priority
- fixed or flexible
- money cost or linked goal
- location/travel time
- notes

## Categories
- work/taxi
- car maintenance
- family
- girlfriend/relationship
- errands
- food/rest
- sleep
- learning/QA
- project/FinFlow
- health

## Planning formula
24 hours - elapsed time - sleep - food - errands - travel - rest = available work window.
Available work window * expected ₽/hour = realistic income range.

If financial plan exceeds realistic range, AI must propose moving tasks, splitting goals, or weekly compensation.
