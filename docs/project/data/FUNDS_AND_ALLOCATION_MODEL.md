# FUNDS AND SMART ALLOCATION MODEL

## Starting point
All funds are currently 0 ₽.

## Core funds
- 🔰 Working fund: fuel, Drivee, operating cashflow.
- ❤️ Meetings: relationship/dates/shared spending turnover fund.
- 🎂 Girlfriend work base: supplies, boxes, cakes, business base.
- 🚗 Car/obligation: 45,000 ₽ cycle.
- 🏦 Bank/bankruptcy: 15,000 ₽ cycle.
- 👤 Personal: food, clothes, basic living.
- 🎁 Girlfriend birthday: 50,000 ₽.
- ✈️ Flight/relocation: 300,000 ₽.
- 🛟 Safety cushion.
- 🔧 Chassis/repair: approx. 50,000 ₽.
- ⚡ Mini-goals: temporary purchases and urgent needs.

## Fund types
1. Critical/operating: working fund, fuel, Drivee, oil, safety car repairs.
2. Mandatory obligations: bank, car payment.
3. Safety: cushion, car repair reserve.
4. Relationship/life: meetings, gifts, girlfriend support.
5. Growth/future: relocation, capital, learning.

## Smart allocation
Do not always distribute equally. Sometimes focus 3,000–5,000 ₽ into one critical target if deadline/risk is high.

Allocation modes:
- Equal mode
- Priority mode
- Recovery mode
- Emergency mode
- Buffer build mode
- Goal sprint mode

AI should choose allocation based on deadline, risk, current balances, working fund, current time, realistic income forecast, and obligations.

## v1.6 Addition — smart allocation instead of equal distribution

FinFlow must not blindly split small amounts across all funds every day.
Sometimes it is better to put 3,000–5,000 ₽ into one urgent item than 250 ₽ into many funds.

Allocation depends on:
- deadlines;
- current balances;
- current time;
- work feasibility;
- working fund state;
- car safety/maintenance;
- critical obligations;
- flexible goals.

If the day is late or underfunded, flexible funds pause first. Working ability, critical payments, food, fuel, Drivee and car safety are protected before relocation/birthday/growth goals.
